Privacy Policy (Chismoso)

Effective date: January 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Chismoso browser extension (“Chismoso”, “we”, “us”) collects, how it is used, and how it is processed.

What data we collect

Chismoso may collect and store the following information, depending on features you use:

  • Account & profile data: name, email address, job title/position, role (e.g., Team Member / Administrator / Developer), and KPI text you provide.
  • User-generated content: weekly check-ins, monthly reports, and other text you submit in the extension.
  • Usage and operational data: timestamps such as when reports are submitted (e.g., submitted_at) and “last seen”/activity timestamps.

How we use your data

We use the data above to:

  • Provide the core functionality of the extension (profile setup, reminders, saving and displaying check-ins/reports).
  • Generate and display admin/activity views (e.g., lists of submitted reports).
  • Generate AI-assisted summaries and reports when requested.
  • Maintain service integrity and basic troubleshooting (e.g., identifying and resolving errors).

Where data is stored

Depending on the feature:

  • Local extension storage: Some data may be stored locally in your browser using Chrome extension storage.
  • Backend database (server-side): Chismoso uses a hosted backend service and database to store and retrieve team data (for example: users, weekly check-ins, monthly reports). This enables account-based access and shared admin views.

Backend processing & third-party services

Chismoso uses a backend service to process requests from the extension.

  • AI processing (Gemini): When you use AI-powered features, your inputs (for example, report content) may be sent from the extension to our backend, and the backend may send that content to third-party AI services such as the Gemini API to generate responses/summaries.
  • We only send the information necessary to perform the requested AI task.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share data only:

  • With service providers needed to run the app (e.g., hosting, database, AI provider) to provide the requested functionality.
  • When required by law, or to protect the security and integrity of the service.

Data retention

  • Data stored in the backend may be retained to provide ongoing access to history and admin views.
  • Administrators/Developers may delete individual reports from the Activity feed (server-side). If a user resubmits a report, it will appear again.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data in transit and at rest. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your choices

  • You can update profile fields from the Settings/Profile area.
  • You can export your local data from the extension.
  • If you need deletion or access requests beyond what the UI provides, contact the administrator of your Chismoso deployment.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the maintainer of your Chismoso deployment.
